# YOLOv1
This is a fully trained from scratch with the VOC dataset YOLOv1.

At first, as you will see, I created a YOLO algorithem from scratch with a completely custom backbone and head. However it demonstrated a poor mAP score of 0.02. Afterwards I decided to switch out my custom backbone for the Resnet-18. That achieved a mAP score 0.23. Seeing that much could be learned I decided to train a final neural net which used the Resnet-34 as the backbone. In addition, I also decided to bring in the 2007 VOC dataset which prior I had not been using to train allowing me to use the entire trainval parts of the VOC 2007 and 2012 datasets for training and testing on the 2007 VOC test set. After this in addition to incorporating data augmenation, a learning rate scheduler, and normalization I ended with a mAP of 0.24 on this model which was not trained on the implanted weights of ImageNet.
